ABOUT US

WE ARE ENACTUS

Enactus is a non-profit organization which connects 37 countries, 1730+ university programs, 7200 student members, 550 corporate organization and individual partners that work for the same cause of social entrepreneurship .Here we help the underprivileged members of the society . We bring for them new jobs, provide them new opportunities and connect society and entrepreneurship in a unique way. Coming up with a start up and then carrying the league is easy as it is for personal profit but bringing up with a business that provides profit to the society requires both skill and emotions. Thus, we at enactus build this unique blend of emotions and knowledge. We conduct various projects based on numerous surveys and countless researches which tackles various problems in accordance to the 17 sustainable development goals designed by the united nations leaders for better world.Serving as a member of Enactus is like a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for any student.

OUR AIM

Enactus is about developing the entrepreneurial and enterprising skills of students in a way that benefits their future career and their community. Students from all subjects can take part and together they form their own University teams which they run themselves to develop projects that are sustainable and have economic benefits for others. Projects are aimed at improving the quality of life and standard of living of the target group by empowering them.
